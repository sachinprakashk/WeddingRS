A remote sensing scientist from Kerala, Sachin traded the greenery of Kerala for the bustling city of pearls to map the Earth—and found love along the way! When he’s not uncovering Earth’s secrets from the sky, you’ll catch him exploring new cities, snapping photos, or jamming to his favorite tunes. This down-to-earth guy is loved by all and is overjoyed to have found his perfect match in Roshny!

Roshny

A Hyderabad born Malayalee girl thriving as a Solution Advisor in a reputed auditing firm, Roshny is known for her quick decision-making and clever insights. When she’s not conquering corporate challenges, you’ll find her relaxing at home with her furry friend who is her biggest weakness and a sea of happiness. With an unwavering love for all creatures and a knack for spontaneity, she’s always ready for the next big adventure!
